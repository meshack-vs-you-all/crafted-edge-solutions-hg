API Development & Integration

Robust, secure, and high-performance API solutions designed to power modern web and mobile ecosystems.

Nov 20, 2025
2 minutes read

In today’s interconnected digital landscape, your API is the silent engine driving your business. At Crafted Edge Solutions, we don’t just write endpoints; we architect robust communication layers that ensure your data flows securely and efficiently across platforms.

Building the Backbone of Your Ecosystem

Whether you are powering a consumer mobile app, an internal admin dashboard, or a third-party partner integration, our APIs are built for scale and reliability. We leverage modern frameworks like Django REST Framework and FastAPI to deliver high-throughput systems that can handle thousands of concurrent requests without breaking a sweat.

Our Core API Capabilities:

  • RESTful & GraphQL Architectures: We choose the right standard for your specific data needs, ensuring flexible and efficient querying.
  • Enterprise-Grade Security: Implementing OAuth2, JWT (JSON Web Tokens), and RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) to keep your sensitive data protected.
  • Automated Documentation: Every API we build comes with interactive Swagger/OpenAPI documentation, making developer handoff and third-party integration a breeze.
  • Performance Optimization: Utilizing Redis caching and asynchronous task processing to deliver sub-second response times.

Specialized for the African Context

We understand the unique challenges of the Kenyan and wider African markets. Our API designs are optimized for low-bandwidth environments and seamless local payment integrations.

  • Daraja (M-Pesa) Integration: Secure, callback-verified payment flows for your business.
  • SMS & USSD Gateways: Connecting your digital products to the majority of users who rely on non-smartphone interfaces.
  • Offline-First Strategies: Intelligent data synchronization protocols that ensure your mobile apps remain functional even when connectivity is intermittent.
