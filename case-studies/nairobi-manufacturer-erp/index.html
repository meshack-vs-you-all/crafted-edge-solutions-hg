Case Study: ERP Integration for a Leading Nairobi Manufacturer

How Crafted Edge Solutions optimized operations and improved efficiency for a major Kenyan manufacturing firm through strategic ERP integration.
ERP Integration Manufacturing Kenya Operational Efficiency

Optimizing Operations Through Strategic Engineering

For a leading manufacturer in the Nairobi metropolitan area, operational efficiency was a significant challenge. Their fragmented systems and manual processes were hindering growth and increasing costs.

The Challenge

  • Data Silos: Information was scattered across multiple departments, leading to inconsistencies and delays.
  • Manual Workflows: Reliance on manual data entry increased the risk of errors and slowed down production cycles.
  • Limited Visibility: Lack of real-time insights into inventory, production, and financial performance.

The Crafted Edge Solution

We designed and implemented a custom ERP integration strategy that unified their core business processes:

  • Centralized Data Hub: Unified all departments into a single, secure platform.
  • Automated Workflows: Streamlined production and inventory management through intelligent automation.
  • Real-Time Analytics: Provided a comprehensive dashboard for informed, data-driven decision-making.

The Impact

  • 30% Increase in Efficiency: Production cycles were significantly reduced.
  • 20% Cost Savings: Optimized inventory management and reduced manual labor.
  • Improved Decision-Making: Real-time insights enabled proactive strategic planning.
